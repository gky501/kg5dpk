Repeater Information

BACN operates on the KG5DPK repeater located on Shinall Mountain in West Little Rock.

Operating Notes

Please use good amateur radio operating practices, identify appropriately, and leave pauses between transmissions so other stations can break in if needed.

Net Use

BACN uses the KG5DPK repeater for twice-monthly nets. All licensed amateur radio operators are invited to check in.
