BACN Nets

BACN holds nets twice a month and invites all licensed amateur radio operators to check in.

Twice Monthly

BACN Net

Check in with BACN using the KG5DPK repeater located on Shinall Mountain in West Little Rock.

Repeater
KG5DPK
Frequency
443.475 MHz
Offset
+5.000 MHz
Tone
156.700
Location
Shinall Mountain, West Little Rock

Checking In

When net control asks for check-ins, provide your callsign clearly. Depending on the net format, you may also be asked for your name, location, and whether you have traffic.

Example Check-In

“KG5DPK, Aaron, Little Rock, no traffic.”
