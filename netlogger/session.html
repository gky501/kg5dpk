BACN Club Net Script

Opening / Preamble

Calling all amateur radio operators. This is __________, Net Control for the BACN Club Net, operating on the KG5DPK repeater located on Shinall Mountain in West Little Rock.

The repeater operates on 443.475 MHz, with a positive offset and a tone of 156.7.

The Backup Auxiliary Communications Network, or BACN Club, was created by Metropolitan EMS employees as a place for licensed amateur radio operators with the disaster communications team to share ideas, sharpen skills, and connect with other amateur radio operators in the community.

The BACN Net meets twice a month, on the first and second Mondays at 18:30 Central Time. All licensed operators are welcome to check in. You do not have to be a club member.

Emergency Traffic

Before we begin check-ins and announcements, we will pause for emergency or priority traffic. If you have emergency or priority traffic, please say, “Break for emergency traffic”, and wait to be acknowledged.

If there is any emergency or priority traffic, please call Net Control now.

[Pause for 5–10 seconds.]

Short-Time / On-Duty Check-Ins

Next, we will call for any stations short on time, including those on duty, in transit, or unable to stay for the entire net.

When checking in, please call Net Control first. After that, unkey your microphone and listen for a second or two to make sure you are not doubling with another station. If you do not hear another station, key up and give your call sign phonetically, along with your name, location, and whether you have traffic for the net.

This practice helps minimize doubling and keeps the net running smoothly.

If there are any short-time or on-duty stations, please call now.

General Check-Ins

We will now begin general check-ins. If there are any general check-ins, please call Net Control now.

Announcements

With no more check-ins at this time, we will move to announcements.

[Read announcements, then return for a second round of check-ins.]

Final Round of Check-Ins

We are now taking the final round of check-ins. Please call Net Control, then pause briefly to make sure there is no doubling.

If you would like to check in, please call Net Control now.

Closing

Hearing no further check-ins or traffic, we will close the BACN Club Net at this time and return the repeater to regular amateur use.

Thank you to everyone who checked in, and we hope to hear you back here __________.

This is __________, Net Control for the BACN Club Net, clear.
